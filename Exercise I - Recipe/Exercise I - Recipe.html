~ FRENCH MACAROONS RECIPE ~

This is a recipe on how to make sweet and delicious French Macaroons, but before you start making those delicious macaroons you have to make sure you have all the ingredients the you need

The ingredients that you will need are listed below:
  • Egg Whites
  • Almond Flour
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Cream of Tartar
  • Salt
  • Flavor of Choice
  • Food Coloring

  1. Preperation
    1. Seperate the egg whites from the eggs.
    2. Age the egg whites on a bowl with a cover for 24hrs.
    3. Sift the powdered sugar and almond flour.
    4. Whisk together the powedered suger and almond flour until well-combined.
  2. Preparing the Meringue
    1. Beat the egg whites till foamy and add the cream of tartar and a pinch of salt.
    2. Add sugar gradually while whisking the egg whites till sweet enough to your liking.
    3. Add the flavor and food color of choice to the mix.
    4. Continue to mix until you've reached stiff peaks.
  3. Macaronage
    1. Add the almond flour and powdered sugar to the meringue.
    2. Deflate the batter by scraping the sides and the bottom.
    3. Mix the batter till you can get a figure 8 that ribbons around the spactula.
    4. Once the batter is ready pour it in a piping bag.
  4. Baking the Macaroons
    1. Pipe the batter into a baking sheet "C" motion by squeezing the piping bag.
    2. Firmly bang the pan agaisnt the table to eliminate air bubbles 4 to 5 times.
    3. Let them rest for about 30-60mins till it smooths out while your oven preheats.
    4. The macaroons should take about 10-12mins to bake
  5. Filling the macaroons
    1. Put a filling of your choice on the flat side of the macaroons
    2. Grab another macaroon and put on the macaroon with filling on the flat side
